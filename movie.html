The Pursuit of Happiness

Cast: Will Smith, Thandie Newton, Jaden Smith


What I Learned from the Movie

The Pursuit of Happiness is a powerful reminder that life is full of challenges, but perseverance and determination can lead to success. The main character, Chris Gardner, teaches us the importance of resilience and never giving up, even when things seem impossible. His journey is a testament to the strength of the human spirit and the belief that one can achieve greatness despite overwhelming odds.
