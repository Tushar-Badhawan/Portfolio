Learning Outcomes

Lecture 1:Proactive mind and Personal Introduction

On the very first lecture of P.E.S.E. Practical for Employability Skill Enhancement, we learn about how small activities can help us enhance fast decision making and thinking, we also saw how to train left brain and right brain simply by just thinking uses of things in our surrounding such as pencil, paper. at last our educator mr. Anshuman demostrated how to give an impactful introduction.

Lecture 2: Active Listening, Teamwork, GD and presentation

On second lecture we were instructed to make group of 5-6 students and have a group discussion to chose an idea to pitch and present in front of classmates. We were given task to actively listen and raise questions as investors during other's presentation.

Lecture 3: Public Speaking Skills and Team presentation

On third lecture we learnt how to speak confidently in public, manage stage fright, and how to engage your audience, dividing roles within team.

Lecture 4: Portfolio: Do's and Don'ts

On the fourth lecture our educator displayed portfolio's, explained do's and don'ts to make an effective and impactful portfolio. We were told about various platforms where we can create our own portfolio and also we had a small one on one career objective analysis with our lecturer.
